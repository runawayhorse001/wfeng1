Research Interests¶

I have great interest in Numerical Partial Differential Equation. My research projects involve the following aspects:

  • Data Mining and Machine Learning
  • Big Data Analytics
  • Numerical Methods For Phase-Field Equation
  • Geometric and Algebraic Multigrid Solver
  • Continuous and Discoutinuous Glerkin Method
  • Immersed and Adaptive Finite Element Method
  • Preconditioning Nonlinear Solvers
  • Adaptive Methods
  • Pseudospectral Method
  • Image Denoising

My research statement can be downloaded in ‘PDF’.
